about

I am a Full Stack Web Developer based out of Oakland, CA. I enjoy building strange, pretty things that remind me of unicorns (but I can build serious, corporate things, too). When I'm not coding, you can find me conducting pizza experiments, singing at the top of my lungs, and traveling globally and other-worldly. This is how I work.

I think inquisitively and creatively about the goal of my projects.

I consider issues from multiple perspectives to ensure that thinkers of all types will understand the final product. I always ask "How can I make this better? Is there a simpler way?" This leads me to constantly refine project goals to maximize their potential.

I actively seek out edge cases and obsess over product quality.

Ideas are brought to life with a positive purpose: maybe it will fix a problem, inspire you, or make tomorrow a bit easier. That's why I make sure that the software I develop is sturdy, well-planned, and ready to deliver a frictionless user experience.

I lead in the way I would like to be led.

When managing teams, I create strength within groups by enabling each person to do what they naturally do best, while simultaneously supporting their endeavors to learn and grow.
