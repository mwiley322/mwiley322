Project 1 : Lights Lookout

The first project my team and I completed was the Lights Lookout website. After logging in, a user can learn more about the Northern Lights, where to find them, discover the best times and places to view them, and much more.

Project 2: Riverbed App

This is the second project I did with my team. "Riverbed" is an app that let's you find amazing places to swim each summer, and, similarly to Waze, it let's the user check in to warn other users about certain things such as high tide, overcrowdedness, glass in the sand, etc. It is ultimately a community of beach explorers.

Project 3: HikerLuxe

If you enjoy the outdoors, then this app is for you - plain and simple. This app has everything one might need if they are the adventurous type. A user can meet up with other hikers, find cool new places, prepare camping lists specific to a trail, and even take courses on survival techniques!
